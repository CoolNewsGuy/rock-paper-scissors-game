Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors!

To play the game, you have to choose either "Rock", "Paper" or "Scissors".

The computer will randomly choose one of the three.

The rules are simple:
-The game will be of 5 rounds.
-If you win the round, +1 for you.
-If the computer wins the round, +1 for computer.
-The player with the highest score wins!


Note: The input checker is case-insensitive (e.g., Rock = rock, RoCK = rock).

If you are ready to play, Click the button below!
